# Demat African Kitchen – Website

This is the official website for Demat African Kitchen in Doha, Qatar. It showcases our authentic African dishes, soups, sides, and drinks with a clean, modern and mobile‑friendly design. Visitors can explore the full menu, browse categories, see prices and portions, and quickly place orders via WhatsApp.

## What you can do on the website

- View our menu with photos, descriptions, and prices
- Switch between categories like Soups & Stews, Swallows, Rice, Sides, and Drinks
- Tap “Order Now” on any item to message us instantly on WhatsApp
- Find our opening hours and contact details
- Follow our Instagram for updates and specials
